Welcome to MUSILAND

ABOUT US

Welcome to Musiland, your trusted online destination in Botswana for buying, renting, and selling musical instruments. Whether you're a beginner, expert, an artist, business, manufacturer, or simply looking to pass your instrument on, we make the process simple, secure, and affordable. Our mission is to connect musicians across the country and keep the music playing one instrument at a time.


THE PROCESS

Getting started is easy! Browse our wide selection of musical instruments to buy or rent, we have a wide category of instruments choose from such as guitars, drums, ukuleles, marimbas, keyboards, etc, etc. Each listing include a detailed description, photos, and seller ratings so you can shop with confidence. Simply add your item to the cart, choose your payment or rental plan, and we will take care of the rest and delivery right to your door in 3 days or less. Looking to sell or rent out your own instrument? Just create a free account, upload your instrument details and photos, set your price, and go live in minutes. We help you connect with interested buyers or renters across Bostwana, and you manage the schedule that works best for you.
